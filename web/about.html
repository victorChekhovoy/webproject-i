About This Data

This data on traffic fatalities comes from two data sets. The first counts the number of annual fatalities for various classes of vehicles, and the second tracks the number of miles driven annually by various classes of vehicles. We combined these data sets to find the average number of Occupant Fatalities per billion miles traveled for each vehicle type. This data covers the date range between 1975 and 2019, although from 1975 to 1990 data was logged every 5 years. We are required by the terms of this data’s licensing to make it clear that our data was obtained from the United States Bureau of Transportation Statistics.
